Emolas was born into a family of thieves and scoundrels, learning the art of stealth and deception from a young age. As he honed his skills, he discovered a latent magical ability that allowed him to tap into the shadows, further enhancing his rogue abilities. Growing up on the streets, Emolas developed a strong sense of self-reliance and cunning.

Some key personality traits of Emolas include:
* He is highly resourceful and able to think on his feet, often relying on his wit and charm to talk his way out of sticky situations.
* Emolas has a strong sense of loyalty to those he considers friends, and will go to great lengths to protect and support them.
* Despite his roguish nature, Emolas has a certain code of honor and will not betray others or engage in activities that go against his personal moral compass.

As for the spell, here is a potential option:
"Shadow Veil: Emolas can cast this spell to become shrouded in shadows, gaining disadvantage on damage attacks against him for 2 turns. While hidden, Emolas can move up to his speed without revealing his position, allowing him to reposition and strike from the shadows. The spell requires concentration and can be broken if Emolas takes damage or uses an ability that requires him to reveal his position."
